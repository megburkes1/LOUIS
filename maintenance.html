Maintenance Instructions

Brush

When to Replace:

  • For reference, it was first used in February of 2025.
  • As a general rule of thumb, replace every 6-12 months. The manufacturer recommended checking its condition frequently and record replacements to track future replacements, as lifespan varies with usage and environmental conditions.
  • Replace if bristles are worn, damaged, or frayed, or if the brush is ineffective at debris removal.

Maintenance Tips:

  • Check the brush weekly for debris buildup, especially after heavy use.
  • Clean it whenever you notice significant buildup.

How to Clean:

  • Rinse with water, preferably demineralized.
  • If needed, use a mild, non-abrasive detergent and a soft cloth.
  • Let the brush dry completely before reattaching.
  • Inspect for worn bristles that may scratch the solar panel.

Motors

When to Replace:

  • The wheel motors were first used in February of 2024, while the stepper and gear motors were first used in February of 2025.
  • Replace every 4-6 years or if there are signs of failure (e.g., losing torque, excessive noise).

Maintenance Tips:

  • Lubricate bearings and rails regularly.
  • Ensure proper alignment and mounting.
  • Clean debris buildup.
  • Inspect wiring and connections for wear or corrosion.
  • Reapply Electro-Seal every 2 years when the rover is powered off to the stepper and gear motors.
